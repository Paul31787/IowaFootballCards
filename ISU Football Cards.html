Cy

"Cardinals are known to fiercely defend their territories and Cy is top level when it comes to defending Iowa State University."

  • Age: Sixty Eight Years Old
  • Terrority: Ames, Iowa
  • Rival: Iowa Hawkeyes
  • Known For: 2007 & 2008 Macot Champ

Through glorious victories and difficult defeats, Cy has been boosting the spirits of the Iowa State University Cyclones for nearly 70 years. More then a Mascot, Cy has been a true leader that when greatness is needed to overcome the fear of failure, to stand up, face those fears, overcome challenges and let nothing hold you back.

Trophy

"The Cyclones were the winner of the 2022 CyHawk Trophy, a long standing rivarly between the two schools."

  • History: Created in 1976
  • About: The Cyclones and Hawkeyes rivarly is celebrated with the trophy each year.

The CyHawk Trophy is awarded to the yearly winner between the Cyclones and the Hawkeyes. A worth while symbol of victory to keep traditions for those that came before, and those who have yet to arrive. A badge of honor or defeat for an entire season, it will have you boasting with pride, or hungry for revenge.

Herky

"Hawks seek to hunt the competition and Herky is not afraid to take a chance and grasp at victory."

  • Age: Sixty Three Years Old
  • Terrority: Iowa City, Iowa
  • Rival: ISU Cyclones
  • Known for: The leader of Kinnick Stadium

A hawk not like any other, Herky is an ambassador for the University of Iowa. The pride of Kinnick stadium and a friend to students, faculty, alumni and the Iowa City community. Always there when needed, ready to lead, ready to serve or just ready to listen. Most importantly, ready to inspire the fans of the Hawkeyes athletics.
